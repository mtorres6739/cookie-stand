About Pat's Salmon Cookies

Welcome to Pat's Salmon Cookies! Here you will find a source of information on how Pat started his business selling Salmon Cookies. You will learn about his journey from being a simple fisherman until one day, he stumbled on a secret recipe hidden deep inside a fish. It was once rumored that the fish was an original pet from the beings who occupied the lost city of Atlantis. Some also think that the recipe came from his great great grandmother. Regardless of where the recipe is from, the cookies are amazing!
